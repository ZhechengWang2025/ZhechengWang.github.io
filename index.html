简历 | Biography

王哲诚于2014年获得中国郑州大学工学学士学位，2017年获得德国慕尼黑工业大学理学硕士学位，，2018年获得同济大学工程硕士学位，2024年获得中国西北工业大学信息与通信工程博士学位。他目前是中国青海大学西宁校区计算机技术与应用学院的讲师。他的研究兴趣包括计算机视觉、点云压缩、视频编码、图像压缩和多媒体通信。

Zhecheng Wang received the B.E. degree from Zhengzhou University, China, in 2014, the M.S. degree from the Technical University of Munich, Germany, in 2017, the M.Eng. degree from the Tongji University, China, in 2018, and the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China, in 2024. He is currently a Lecturer at the School of Computer Technology and Application, Qinghai University, Xining, China. His research interests include computer vision, point cloud compression, video coding, image compression, and multimedia communication.

Zhecheng Wang, Ph.D. in Engineering, currently serves as a Lecturer at Qinghai University. His main research interests include computer vision, multimedia data processing, compression coding and decoding, and storage. He has achieved multiple innovative research results in fields such as point cloud compression and geometric coding, with numerous papers published in top international journals including IEEE Transactions on Multimedia and IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology.

As a key contributor to the development of the G-PCC international standard, he has submitted several technical proposals that have been adopted by both the first and second generations of the G-PCC standard. He has led and participated in multiple enterprise collaborative research projects, maintaining close partnerships with companies such as Inspur, OPPO, and Shanghai Hisilicon.
